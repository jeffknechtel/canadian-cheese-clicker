# The Great Canadian Cheese Quest - Community Setup Guide

## Overview

This guide outlines recommendations for establishing community channels to support The Great Canadian Cheese Quest. Strong community engagement is essential for an indie game's success, providing feedback, building loyalty, and generating organic word-of-mouth marketing.

### Channel Descriptions

#### Information Channels
- **#welcome**: Auto-message for new members with rules overview and role selection
- **#rules**: Community guidelines, zero tolerance for toxicity
- **#announcements**: Official updates only (locked to mods)
- **#faq**: Pinned frequently asked questions
- **#changelog**: Automatic posts from game updates

#### Community Channels
- **#general-chat**: Main social area
- **#introductions**: New member introductions
- **#off-topic**: Non-game discussion
- **#memes-and-humor**: Canadian cheese memes encouraged

#### Gameplay Channels
- **#tips-and-strategies**: Gameplay advice and discoveries
- **#hero-builds**: Party compositions and hero equipment
- **#crafting-recipes**: Cheese recipe discussions
- **#prestige-discussion**: Optimal prestige timing and strategies
- **#achievements**: Achievement hunting and completionism

#### Support Channels
- **#bug-reports**: Structured bug report template
- **#feature-requests**: Community suggestions
- **#technical-help**: Performance and compatibility issues

### Roles

#### Staff Roles
- **Developer**: Game developers (special color)
- **Community Manager**: Official community team
- **Moderator**: Volunteer moderators

#### Community Roles
- **Beta Tester**: Participated in beta
- **Cheese Master**: Prestige tier 3 achieved
- **Content Creator**: Streamers/YouTubers
- **Contributor**: Significant community contributions

#### Engagement Roles (Auto-assigned)
- **Fresh Curd**: New member
- **Aged Cheddar**: 1 month active
- **Vintage Gouda**: 6 months active
- **Legacy Brie**: 1 year active

### Bots Recommended
- **MEE6 or Carl-bot**: Moderation and leveling
- **Dyno**: Auto-roles and custom commands
- **Statbot**: Server analytics

### Moderation Guidelines
1. Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
2. No spam or self-promotion outside designated channels
3. Keep discussions civil and constructive
4. Spoiler tags for major discoveries
5. No cheating/exploit discussion (use #bug-reports instead)

## Reddit Subreddit

### Subreddit Setup

**Name**: r/CanadianCheeseQuest or r/CheeseQuestGame

### Rules Sidebar
1. Be respectful to all community members
2. No spam or excessive self-promotion
3. Use appropriate flairs for posts
4. Spoiler tag major discoveries
5. No discussion of cheats or exploits
6. Bug reports should include game version
7. Feature requests should be constructive
8. Search before posting duplicate topics

### Post Flairs
- **Discussion**: General game discussion
- **Strategy**: Tips, guides, builds
- **Achievement**: Milestone celebrations
- **Bug Report**: Issue reports
- **Feature Request**: Suggestions
- **Fan Art**: Community creations
- **Media**: Screenshots and videos
- **Official**: Developer posts
- **Meme**: Humor and memes (limited to specific days)

### AutoModerator Rules
- Remove posts without flair after 10 minutes
- Sticky weekly discussion threads
- Filter spam accounts (low karma, new accounts)
- Auto-reply to common questions with FAQ links

### Scheduled Posts
- **Monday**: Megathread - Questions and Help
- **Wednesday**: Show-off Thread - Achievements
- **Friday**: Fan Art Friday
- **Saturday**: Meme Saturday (limited humor day)

## Crisis Management

### Issue Response Protocol
1. **Acknowledge**: Respond within 2 hours
2. **Investigate**: Confirm issue details
3. **Communicate**: Post official status update
4. **Resolve**: Implement fix
5. **Follow-up**: Confirm resolution and thank community

### Common Scenarios
- **Game-breaking bug**: Immediate announcement, hotfix priority
- **Server/hosting issues**: Status updates every 30 minutes
- **Community conflict**: Private resolution, public statement if needed
- **Negative review bombing**: Address concerns, don't engage trolls
