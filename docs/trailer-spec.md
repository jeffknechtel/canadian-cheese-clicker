# The Great Canadian Cheese Quest - Trailer Specification

## Overview

This document outlines the specifications for the official launch trailer. The trailer should capture the unique blend of idle clicker satisfaction and JRPG strategic depth while celebrating Canadian culture.

## Technical Specifications

### Video Format
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) primary, 1080p (1920x1080) for web
- **Frame Rate:** 60 FPS
- **Codec:** H.264 (MP4 container)
- **Bitrate:** 50 Mbps (4K), 20 Mbps (1080p)
- **Audio:** AAC, 48kHz, stereo

### Duration
- **Target Length:** 60 seconds
- **Maximum Length:** 90 seconds
- **Minimum Length:** 45 seconds

### Aspect Ratios
- 16:9 for YouTube/main distribution
- 9:16 vertical cut for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- 1:1 square cut for social media

## Trailer Structure

### Act 1: The Hook (0-5 seconds)
**Purpose:** Grab attention immediately

**Visual:**
- Quick zoom into spinning cheese wheel
- Explosion of cheese particle effects
- Brief flash of curd counter increasing rapidly

**Audio:**
- Satisfying click sound
- Rising musical note
- "The Great Canadian Cheese Quest" title splash

**Text Overlay:**
- Game title with maple leaf accent

### Act 2: Core Gameplay (5-20 seconds)
**Purpose:** Show the idle clicker foundation

**Visual Sequence:**
1. (5-8s) Player clicking cheese wheel - particles flying
2. (8-12s) Generator panel filling - automation in action
3. (12-16s) Numbers going up - satisfying progression
4. (16-20s) Currency display celebrating milestones

**Audio:**
- Upbeat Canadian-folk inspired music begins
- Click sounds layered under music
- Achievement unlock chime

**Text Overlays:**
- "Click. Produce. Prosper."
- "30+ Generators"
- "Endless Progression"

### Act 3: Combat System (20-35 seconds)
**Purpose:** Reveal the JRPG depth

**Visual Sequence:**
1. (20-23s) Transition zoom into combat zone
2. (23-27s) Party of 4 heroes facing boss
3. (27-30s) ATB bars filling, hero abilities activating
4. (30-33s) Special ability animation - particle explosion
5. (33-35s) Victory screen with rewards

**Audio:**
- Music shifts to combat theme (faster tempo)
- Ability sound effects
- Dramatic hit sounds
- Victory fanfare

**Text Overlays:**
- "Recruit 30+ Heroes"
- "Strategic ATB Combat"
- "Epic Boss Battles"

### Act 4: Canadian Content Montage (35-50 seconds)
**Purpose:** Showcase breadth and Canadian theme

**Visual Sequence:**
1. (35-38s) Province map showing all 13 regions
2. (38-41s) Quick cuts of different zone environments
3. (41-44s) Hero recruitment gallery scroll
4. (44-47s) Crafting panel - cheese recipes
5. (47-50s) Prestige animation - golden rennet

**Audio:**
- Music builds with Canadian cultural instruments
- Ambient sounds from different provinces
- Prestige whoosh effect

**Text Overlays:**
- "13 Provinces to Explore"
- "Canadian Mythology"
- "50+ Cheese Recipes"
- "Triple Prestige System"

### Act 5: Call to Action (50-60 seconds)
**Purpose:** Drive action

**Visual Sequence:**
1. (50-53s) Quick montage of highlights
2. (53-56s) Logo animation with maple leaf
3. (56-60s) "Play Now Free" with platforms

**Audio:**
- Music crescendo and resolve
- Logo sound sting

**Text Overlays:**
- "Play Now - Free!"
- Website URL
- Platform icons

## Music Specifications

### Main Theme Requirements
- Style: Canadian folk-inspired, warm and inviting
- Tempo: 120-130 BPM for upbeat sections
- Instruments: Acoustic guitar, fiddle hints, light percussion
- Mood: Cozy yet adventurous

### Combat Theme Requirements
- Style: Orchestral with folk elements
- Tempo: 140-150 BPM
- Instruments: Strings, brass, driving percussion
- Mood: Exciting, strategic tension

### Transition Considerations
- Music should have clear build points at:
  - 5 seconds (title reveal)
  - 20 seconds (combat transition)
  - 50 seconds (final build)

## Text Style Guide

### Font
- Title: Bold serif with Canadian character
- Body: Clean sans-serif, high readability
- All text white with subtle drop shadow for visibility

### Animation
- Text should fade in (0.3s ease)
- Hold for minimum 2 seconds
- Fade out or cut with scene change

### Localization Consideration
- Leave space for longer translations
- Keep text concise (5 words max per overlay)

## Accessibility Considerations

- Include audio descriptions track
- Ensure text meets contrast requirements
- Provide closed captions/subtitles
- Avoid rapid flashing (photosensitivity)
- Include alt-text for thumbnail

## Distribution Versions

### YouTube (Primary)
- 4K and 1080p versions
- Custom thumbnail (1280x720)
- End screen compatible (last 20 seconds)

### Social Media Cuts
- 30-second highlight reel
- 15-second teaser (focus on hook + combat)
- 6-second bumper ad

### Vertical Versions
- 60-second vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels
- 15-second vertical short

## Thumbnail Specifications

### Main Thumbnail (YouTube)
- Size: 1280x720 pixels
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Content: Cheese wheel, hero character, game title
- Style: Bright, high contrast, readable at small sizes

### Social Thumbnails
- Square: 1080x1080
- Story/Vertical: 1080x1920
